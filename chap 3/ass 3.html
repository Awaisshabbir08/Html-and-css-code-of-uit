Muffin Recipe

A basic Recipe. You can add pretty much anything to it!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 200g caster sugar
  • 250ml milk
  • 125ml vegetable oil
  • 400g plain flour
  • 3tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt

Method

  1. Preheat oven to gas mark 6 or a medium temperature.Line muffin tray with paper muffin cases
  2. Beat the eggs in a mixing bowl and then mix in the oil and milk.Add the sugar and the whisk until dissolved.
  3. Shift in the dry ingredients:flour , Baking Powder and salt and mix until smooth.NB:add any extra ingredients now eg. chocolate chips/fruit.
  4. Fill muffin cases TWO THIRDS full(this is important for a perfect size muffins) and bake in the middle of the oven for 25 minutes (approx. depending on your oven)
  5. Leave to cool (or enjoy warm!)
